How Milaap Works ?

  • Register: Admin of family will register here.
  • Login: Admin will login here after that it will be moved to Dashboard where they can search or add their family members.
  • Add Member: To add family members to the database.
  • Search Member: Segment to scan the face of lost child and then send the authentication request to lost child parent.
  • All Members: To visit all members of family.
  • Child Details : Here you can see the details after authentication from lost child's parent.

About Us

  • Milaap is a web application which helps people to find the family of the lost child in crowded area using face recognition with secure two factor authentication.
  • Built using Django, OpenCV and Sqlite3 Database for storing the information of the family.
  • Scan the image of the lost child using of webcam or mobile camera then it shows the similar results from the database.
  • Genrate & Send a information access request containing cordinates of requester to family head to authenticate the absence of his/her child.
  • After authentication grant, requester able to access contact details of family.
  • Helps in recognizing the information of the people who are caught in any accident or in natural calamity.
